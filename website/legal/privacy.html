Privacy Policy & Data Usage

Effective date: 2026-01-27

Questions or requests: support@feedverse.app

Overview

Feedverse (“we”, “us”) is a roleplay app where you create scenarios, character profiles, posts, and messages. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect:

  • Account data: email address, username, and authentication/session information.
  • Profile content: character names, bios, avatars, and other profile fields you provide.
  • Scenario and post content: posts, comments, reactions, and other content you create.
  • Messages: direct messages and related metadata needed to deliver them.
  • Technical data: device/app info and basic logs (for security, abuse prevention, and reliability).

How we use information

  • To provide and maintain the service (accounts, scenarios, posts, DMs).
  • To secure the service (fraud prevention, rate limiting, account recovery).
  • To communicate with you about verification and support requests.
  • To improve reliability and user experience.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers who help us run Feedverse (for example, email delivery). These providers are used only to operate the service.

Data retention

We retain information as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. If you request deletion, we will delete your account data subject to any required retention.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

  • Access/updates: you can update certain information in-app (where available).
  • Deletion: see the Data Deletion page for instructions.

Children’s privacy

Feedverse is not intended for children under 13 (or the age required by local law). If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the effective date above when changes are made.
