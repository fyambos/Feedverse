Data Deletion

Effective date: 2026-01-27

Email deletion requests to support@feedverse.app

How to request deletion

To request deletion of your Feedverse account and associated data, email us from the address on your account.

  • To: support@feedverse.app
  • Subject: Delete my Feedverse account
  • Include: your username and any relevant details (for example, if you changed emails).

What we delete

We will delete your account and personal identifiers where feasible. Some content may remain in scenarios if it is part of shared story history; in those cases, we may anonymize content or remove personal identifiers.

Timeline

We aim to process deletion requests within a reasonable time. In some cases, we may need additional time to verify the request or comply with legal obligations.
